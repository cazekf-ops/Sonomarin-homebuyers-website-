FAQs — Selling Your House Fast in Santa Rosa

How fast can you buy my house in Santa Rosa?

We can close in as little as 7 days if the title is clear. Most sellers prefer 2–3 weeks to give themselves time to make arrangements. Either way, we close on your schedule — not ours.

Do I need to clean or make repairs before selling?

Not at all. We buy Santa Rosa homes in any condition — whether it's move-in ready, heavily damaged, or full of belongings left behind. Leave everything as-is. We handle cleanup and repairs ourselves after closing.

Are there any fees or commissions involved?

Zero fees. Zero commissions. We even cover closing costs. The offer we make you is the amount you walk away with — no surprises at the closing table.

How do you determine what to offer for my Santa Rosa home?

We look at recent comparable sales in Santa Rosa, the current condition of your property, and the cost of any work we'll need to do. We walk every property personally — we don't make sight-unseen lowball offers. We aim to be fair and transparent about how we arrive at our number.

What if I'm not sure selling for cash is the right move?

That's completely fine — we'll tell you honestly. If we think listing on the MLS would get you significantly more money and you have time to wait, we'll say so. Our goal is to help you make the best decision for your situation, even if that means not selling to us.
